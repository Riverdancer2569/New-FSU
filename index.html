Veterans & Military Services
Mission Statement

Assist Military Connected Students in achieving their educational and professional goals while providing support and assistance for a smooth transition from the military to the academic environment.

Collaborations with Intra-University Departments and throughout the Metro-West and Greater Boston communities support providing specific aid and referrals to overcome the unique obstacles Military Connected Students may be faced with.
